THE AKAN.

Brief Desription.

This page takes the user's birthday information and using a formular calculates the day of the week they were born.
This page also allows the user to input their gender from which it outputs their Akan name.

The Akan names are derived from a Ghanian culture,these are first names given to children which corresponds
to the day of the week they were born.
